Safety at Vector Rideshare

At Vector, the safety of our riders and drivers is our highest priority. Check out all the ways we're bringing integrity and safety back to Ride Sharing.

Only .Edu

Only .edu means that only people with a .edu email address can access the site. Our app features a closed network, further securing your data and privacy. This means that all users, including drivers, will be students, parents or trusted faculty, helping ensure the safety of our app.​

Social Media Integration

Drivers not only have a profile on our site, but are required to post links to their social media accounts before taking any rides with Vector. We also have messaging available on our app, so that Vectoreans can talk to prospective drivers to see what they’re like. ​

Tri-Vet Security System

We ensure our riders’ safety through a tri-vet security screening, incorporating social media integration and driving records checks. Vectoreans can relax knowing their drivers have been fully vetted by our staff.

Public Drop-offs

All of our rides end in a populated, public transit area where it’s easy to get to your final destination. These places include train and bus stations, as well as city centers.
